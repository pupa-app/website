Why Pupa

Your agentic experience, made to last

So much of what you do with an agent is lost the moment the conversation ends — trapped in unstructured text, or scattered across the dozen services you stitch together. We keep pushing agents toward more structure, yet the results stay fragile and fragmented.

▾

Pupa changes that. Whatever your setup — a single plain ChatGPT thread or an elaborate custom agentic pipeline — your same agent now can, by default, use and build a real app alongside you. And that app stays with you: Pupa gives it a standard to persist, travel, and stay robust long after the chat is gone and the harness changes.

It fits either way: add a Pupa app on top of the agentic setup you already have, or make it the main app you use — with the agent just helping out.

And a Pupa app is just a JSON file that Pupa connects to your backend agent. Nothing is locked in, so it’s fully portable and works with any harness.
